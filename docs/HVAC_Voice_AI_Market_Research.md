## 2. Common Features Included

### Core Features (Standard across most providers)

- **24/7 Call Answering** -- No voicemail, instant pickup, unlimited concurrent calls
- **Appointment Booking** -- Real-time scheduling integrated with calendar/FSM software
- **After-Hours Answering** -- Primary use case; captures calls when office is closed
- **Lead Qualification** -- Captures name, service type, address, urgency level, budget
- **Emergency Call Triage** -- Detects keywords like "no heat," "no cooling," "gas smell," "water leak" and escalates immediately
- **Call Transfer / Escalation** -- Routes emergencies to on-call technicians in real-time
- **SMS/Email Notifications** -- Sends summaries to business owner and dispatchers
- **Multilingual Support** -- Typically English and Spanish; some support 10+ languages

### Advanced Features (Premium tiers)

- **CRM/FSM Integration** -- Bi-directional sync with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber
- **Customer History Lookup** -- Recognizes returning customers, pulls service agreements
- **Automated Confirmations & Reminders** -- Sends booking confirmations and appointment reminders via SMS
- **Post-Service Follow-Up** -- Automated satisfaction calls after service completion
- **Call Recording & Transcription** -- 99.8% transcription accuracy; call summaries delivered via email
- **CSR Performance Coaching** -- AI reviews calls and provides actionable coaching insights (Avoca)
- **Service Area Qualification** -- Verifies caller is within service territory before booking
- **Voicemail Transcription** -- Converts any voicemails to text with email/SMS delivery
- **Multi-Channel** -- Voice, SMS, and chat from a single platform
- **Outbound Campaigns** -- Appointment reminders, maintenance plan renewals, seasonal tune-up promos

### HVAC-Specific Capabilities

- Emergency keyword detection and priority routing
- HVAC troubleshooting guidance (e.g., "check your thermostat settings")
- Equipment/system detail collection before dispatch
- Property access instruction gathering
- Seasonal demand surge handling (300% call spikes on first 90F+ day)
- Maintenance agreement upselling during calls

## 3. Average Call Volume for Small-to-Medium HVAC Businesses

### Baseline Estimates

| Business Stage / Size | Monthly Inbound Calls | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Startup (Month 1-3)** | 30-40 calls/mo | 8-10 calls first month, ramping to 30-40 |
| **Early Growth (Month 4-6)** | 40-60 calls/mo | Word of mouth building |
| **Established Small (1-2 trucks)** | 100-200 calls/mo | ~150 calls/mo is a common benchmark |
| **Established Medium (3-5 trucks)** | 300-500 calls/mo | ~400 calls/mo commonly used in industry models |
| **Larger Operation (6-10 trucks)** | 600-1,000 calls/mo | ~800 calls/mo benchmark |

### Seasonal Variation

- **Peak season (summer heat waves, winter cold snaps)**: Call volume can spike 200-300% above average
- **First day temperatures exceed 90F**: Highest call volume day of the year
- **Off-season**: Can drop 40-60% below peak

### Key Call Statistics

- **27% of calls to home services businesses go unanswered** (industry average)
- **Less than 3% of callers who reach voicemail leave a message** -- the rest call a competitor
- **62% of consumers call before making a purchase** in home services
- **76% will stop doing business with a company after one negative experience**
- **Average call duration**: ~3 minutes for scheduling, 5-7 minutes for troubleshooting

### Call Volume by Type (Typical HVAC Breakdown)

- Scheduling / appointment requests: ~40%
- Service inquiries / pricing questions: ~25%
- Emergency calls: ~15%
- Existing appointment changes/cancellations: ~10%
- Warranty / maintenance agreement questions: ~10%

## 5. ROI Arguments for HVAC Companies Adopting Voice AI

### The Missed Call Problem

- **27% of calls to HVAC businesses go unanswered** (industry average)
- **Less than 3% of callers leave voicemails** -- the other 97% call a competitor
- **Average HVAC service call value: $300-500**; emergency calls: $900+
- **Missing just 2 calls per week = $31,000 - $52,000 lost annually**
- **At 100 calls/month with 27% miss rate = 27 missed opportunities = $21,600/month in potential lost revenue**

### Direct Financial ROI

**Example 1 -- Small HVAC Company:**
- AI voice agent cost: ~$236/month
- Average service call revenue: $400
- Needs only 1 additional booked job per month to break even
- Capturing just 5 previously-missed calls = $2,000/month additional revenue
- **ROI: ~750% in first year**

**Example 2 -- Dallas HVAC Company (4 trucks, real case study):**
- Before AI: 180 weekly calls, 78 missed (43% miss rate)
- After AI: 180 weekly calls, 6 missed (3% miss rate)
- Additional jobs booked: 44 per week
- Additional weekly revenue: $19,800
- Additional monthly revenue: $79,200
- AI monthly cost: $2,400
- Staff reduction savings: $3,200/month
- **Net monthly gain: ~$80,000**
- **ROI: 3,233%**
- System paid for itself in 11 bookings

**Example 3 -- Phoenix HVAC Company:**
- Missed call rate: reduced from 37% to 3%
- Monthly revenue increase: $64,000

### Labor Cost Comparison

| Metric | Human Receptionist | AI Voice Agent |
|---|---|---|
| Hourly cost | $18-25/hr | ~$0.10-0.30/call |
| Availability | 8-10 hours/day, weekdays | 24/7/365 |
| Concurrent calls | 1 at a time | Unlimited |
| Annual cost | $37,000-52,000/yr + benefits | $3,000-7,500/yr |
| Consistency | Variable | 100% consistent |
| Sick days / turnover | Yes | No |

### Key Selling Points for HVAC Owners

1. **"Never miss a call again"** -- 24/7 coverage including nights, weekends, holidays
2. **Peak season scalability** -- Handle 300% call spikes without hiring temp staff
3. **After-hours revenue capture** -- Emergencies happen at 2am; the first company to answer gets the job
4. **Reduced overhead** -- Replace or supplement 1-2 office staff ($3,200-6,400/mo savings)
5. **Faster response time** -- No hold times; instant pickup every time
6. **Professional consistency** -- Every call handled the same way, every time
7. **Spanish-language support** -- Expand serviceable market without bilingual hires
8. **Marketing ROI protection** -- Stop paying for Google Ads that ring to voicemail
9. **Owner work-life balance** -- Stop answering calls at dinner and on weekends
10. **Competitive advantage** -- 76% of consumers will switch to whoever answers first

### Payback Period

- Most HVAC businesses achieve payback within **1-3 months**
- Even conservative estimates show payback within **3-6 months**
- One saved emergency call per month ($900+) more than covers most AI service costs

## Key Takeaways for Pricing Your HVAC Voice AI Service

1. **Sweet spot pricing for SMB HVAC**: $297-497/month positions well against competitors
2. **Setup fees of $2,000-3,500 are standard** and accepted by HVAC business owners
3. **Break-even is easy to demonstrate**: 1-2 additional jobs/month covers the cost
4. **After-hours is the #1 selling point**: This is where HVAC companies lose the most revenue
5. **ServiceTitan integration is a differentiator**: Many HVAC companies use it and want seamless connectivity
6. **Emergency triage is table stakes**: Any HVAC voice AI must handle "no heat" and "gas leak" scenarios
7. **Seasonal flexibility matters**: HVAC owners want to know it can handle summer/winter surges
